About the Founder

Daniel Knight

Founder & Systems Architect at Knight Ops

For more than two decades, I've been the person organizations call when their operations hit a wall. Not for advice — for the system that breaks through it.

I've built custom systems for multi-billion-dollar corporations, financial advisory firms managing hundreds of millions, government programs distributing millions in aid, 12-dealership automotive groups competing nationally, and founder-led businesses ready to stop running on duct tape and spreadsheets.

The common thread: every one of these organizations had a bottleneck that no off-the-shelf tool could solve. They needed something built specifically for how they operate. That's what Knight Ops exists to do.

Why I Work Directly With Every Client

When you work with Knight Ops, you work with me. Not a junior developer, not a project manager playing telephone. The person who architects your system is the person who builds it. That's how you get something that actually fits your business instead of a generic template with your logo on it.

What I Believe

The right system at the right time changes everything. It's not about technology — it's about removing the friction between where you are and where you're headed. Every system I build is designed to create leverage: more output with less input, more clarity with less noise, more growth with less chaos.
