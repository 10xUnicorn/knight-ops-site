Can I build a meal prep subscription system without using DoorDash or UberEats?

Yes. DoorDash and UberEats take 15% to 30% of every order, own the customer relationship, and don't support subscription models well. Knight Ops builds you a custom platform where customers order directly from you, subscribe to weekly meal plans, and pay you directly. Zero commission fees, zero middlemen.

How fast can a meal prep ordering system be built?

Knight Ops uses AI-accelerated development workflows, so builds move significantly faster than traditional agencies. Timelines depend on scope, but speed is a consistent advantage. The focus is on getting the system architecture right for your business, and then we move fast on execution.

What features should a meal prep business app include?

A strong meal prep platform should include weekly menu management, subscription plans with auto-renewal, custom order selection, delivery zone and schedule management, payment processing, customer accounts with order history, push notifications for menu updates, and an admin dashboard for order management and prep planning. Knight Ops builds all of these into one custom platform.

Is a custom app better than using Square or Toast for a meal prep business?

Square and Toast are designed for in-store point-of-sale, not subscription meal prep. They don't handle weekly ordering, delivery scheduling, or recurring subscriptions well. A custom Knight Ops build is purpose-built for how meal prep businesses actually operate: weekly menus, subscription management, delivery logistics, and direct customer relationships with zero platform fees.
