Financial Advisory
Dashboard Automation Financial Services
THE PROBLEM

A financial advisory firm managing a $100M book spent 30 minutes preparing for every single client review. Multiply that across hundreds of clients, and the advisor was drowning in prep work instead of building relationships.

THE SOLUTION

Knight Ops built a custom dashboard that pulls every relevant data point — portfolio performance, life events, meeting history, action items — into a single view.

THE RESULT

Prep time dropped from 30 minutes to under 2 minutes. The advisor now walks into every meeting fully prepared and deeply connected.

#1
Automotive
KPI Dashboard Data Unification Automotive
THE PROBLEM

Twelve separate car dealerships. Twelve different reporting systems. Zero visibility into what was actually happening across the group. Leadership was making decisions based on guesswork.

THE SOLUTION

Knight Ops built a unified KPI dashboard that consolidated every dealership's sales, service, and customer data into a single command center.

THE RESULT

For the first time, leadership could see performance in real time, spot underperformers instantly, and allocate resources where they mattered. The group went from middle-of-the-pack to the #1 performing region nationally.

Government Programs
Rapid Deploy Forms & Automation Government
THE PROBLEM

When the CARES Act launched, organizations needed to process aid applications immediately. The problem: everything was on paper, and the volume was overwhelming.

THE SOLUTION

Knight Ops built and deployed a complete paper-to-digital application system in 24 hours.
