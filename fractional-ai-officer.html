Common Questions

The honest answers before you ask.

Is this for me if I'm not running AI in my business yet?+
Probably not yet. The Fractional AI Officer assumes you have systems to optimize. If you're starting from scratch, do a Blueprint Call first to map what to build, then a Night Build to ship the foundation. Once that's live, we layer the FAO retainer on top.
What if we don't use all our build credits one month?+
Unused hours roll over up to 1.5× your monthly allotment, so you can save them for a sprint. Beyond that they expire — but in practice almost every client uses everything every month.
How is this different from hiring you for a Night Build?+
Night Build is a one-time project. The FAO retainer is ongoing leadership + capacity. You'd want the FAO when you have systems live and need them maintained, optimized, and expanded continuously — not just shipped.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. 30-day notice. No long-term contract. You own all code, all data, all agents — even after you cancel. We hand off everything.
Do you sign NDAs?+
Yes. Mutual NDA available. We handle financial models to private client data with appropriate care.
Can you work with my existing dev team?+
Yes. The FAO is often the strategic layer that bridges your existing team + AI capability. We mentor your devs, document our architecture, and split work as makes sense.
How quickly can we start?+
Same week. Click Start, complete a brief onboarding form, and we'll schedule your first strategy call within 3 business days. First build credits start the same week.
