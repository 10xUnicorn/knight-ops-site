Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you decide.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?

+

Primarily founder-led service businesses — consultants, coaches, agencies, financial advisory firms, community owners, and event businesses. If you have real revenue and real operational pain, we're likely a good fit. We work best with people who value relationships, care about doing things right, and are ready to invest in infrastructure that lasts.

What's the difference between the Discovery Call and the Blueprint?

+

The Discovery Call (free, 30 min) is exploratory — we listen, identify your biggest bottleneck, and give you honest feedback on what's possible. The Blueprint Session ($750, 90 min) is a deep working session where we architect your actual system together in real time. You'll see your vision take shape during the call, walk out with a complete implementation plan, and be able to decide with full clarity whether you want us to build it. The $750 is applied to your project if you move forward.

Do I need to know exactly what I want built?

+

Not at all. Most clients come with a problem, not a solution — and that's the ideal starting point. Our process is designed to translate your operational pain into a clear, structured system plan. You bring the vision; we handle the architecture.

Can I keep the Blueprint even if I don't hire Knight Ops?

+

Absolutely. The Blueprint Session delivers a standalone implementation plan you own forever. Take it to any developer or team. But if you move forward with Knight Ops, your $750 is applied directly toward the build — so there's zero risk in starting here.

How fast can you build?

+

Our fastest deployment was 24 hours. Most projects are scoped in days to weeks, not months. Speed comes from clarity — and the Blueprint Session ensures we're aligned on exactly what to build before we start. No wasted time, no scope creep, no surprises.

What types of systems can you build?

+

Dashboards, client portals, CRMs, onboarding automations, reporting tools, AI-powered internal tools, affiliate systems, event platforms, community hubs, and custom applications for unique business processes. If it solves a real business problem and creates leverage, we can build it.
