The Math

What Fractional AI Officer Services Actually Cost (and Save)

The math behind fractional AI officer services is straightforward. Here is what the same outcomes cost through other approaches:

  • Full-time Chief AI Officer: $200K to $400K per year in salary, plus benefits, equity, recruiting costs, and a 3 to 6 month hiring timeline. Most businesses under $10M in revenue cannot justify this.
  • Stack of SaaS tools: $1,200 or more per month for tools like Jasper, Drift, Zapier, and various AI plugins. The tools do not talk to each other, nobody optimizes them, and you are renting someone else's infrastructure.
  • Agency project work: $15K to $50K per project with no ongoing optimization. The agency delivers a system, disappears, and you are left maintaining something you did not build.
  • Fractional Chief AI Officer (Knight Ops): Five thousand to eight thousand dollars per month. You get both the strategy and the execution. Weekly sessions, monthly builds, continuous optimization, 100% code ownership. Cancel anytime.

The difference is not just cost. It is that fractional AI officer services combine the strategic thinking of a C-suite leader with the hands-on capability of a senior builder. You do not have to hire separately for strategy and execution.
