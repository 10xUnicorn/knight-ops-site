Common questions about Knight Ops

Quick answers to what people ask most before booking a call. Don't see your question? Email me.

What kinds of businesses do you work with?+
Founder-led service businesses — coaches, consultants, agencies, course creators, mastermind leaders, speakers, financial advisors, and community owners. If you have real revenue and real operational pain, we're likely a great fit.
How fast can you build?+
Our fastest deployment was 4 hours. Most projects are scoped in days to weeks, not months. Speed comes from clarity — we ensure we're aligned on exactly what to build before we start.
Do I need to know exactly what I want built?+
Not at all. Most clients come with a problem, not a solution. Our process translates your operational pain into a clear system plan. You bring the vision; we handle the architecture.
What is the 10x Systems Blueprint Call?+
A free 30-minute working session where you walk away with a live HTML roadmap document mapping your full custom tech stack, every system to implement, build details, and the order to ship them. Yours to keep — whether or not we work together.
How much does it cost?+
Pricing is custom based on your specific build needs. Engagements typically range from $1,497 (Night Launch — overnight HTML site) to $14,997+ (Night Build Pro — full-stack app with iOS + Android App Store deployment). Book a Blueprint Call for a custom scope and quote.
Do you ship to the App Store?+
Yes. Night Build Pro includes full iOS App Store + Google Play submission with compliance review. Your custom app, in the App Store, branded as you, typically in 7–14 days from spec lock.
Do I own the code?+
Yes. Full code ownership is included in every package. You get the GitHub repo, all source files, deployment access, and full handoff documentation. No vendor lock-in, ever.
What tech stack do you use?+
Modern, scalable, and AI-ready by default: Next.js, React, TypeScript, Supabase (Postgres + Auth + Storage + Edge Functions), Vercel hosting, Stripe payments, Resend for email, plus AI integrations via Claude/OpenAI APIs and Model Context Protocol (MCP).
What if we need changes after launch?+
Every package includes a post-launch revision window. Beyond that, we offer fractional engineering retainers and per-revision pricing. You own the code, so any developer can also take it forward.
Can you integrate with my existing tools?+
Yes — Go High Level, Stripe, Calendly, Apollo, ConvertKit, Kajabi, Notion, Airtable, Zapier, Make, Slack, Discord, OpenAI, Claude, Resend, Postmark, Supabase, Firebase, AWS, Vercel, Cloudflare, and almost any modern API. If it has an API, we can wire it.
How do you handle data privacy and security?+
Every project ships with Row-Level Security on the database, encrypted secrets, HTTPS everywhere, secure auth (magic link or password with bcrypt), and audit logs. Sensitive data never lives in client-side code. For regulated industries, we can add HIPAA-ready logging and additional compliance layers.
Can I see examples of past work?+
Yes — every tool you see on knightops.biz was built by us: the AI Systems Audit, the Prospecting Filter Score, the free tools library, the admin command center, and dozens of client builds. The case studies page has more.
