Financial Advisory

Client Review Prep Time — Eliminated

A financial advisory firm managing a $100M book spent 30 minutes preparing for every single client review. Multiply that across hundreds of clients, and the advisor was drowning in prep work instead of building relationships.

Knight Ops built a custom dashboard that pulls every relevant data point — portfolio performance, life events, meeting history, action items — into a single view. Prep time dropped from 30 minutes to under 2 minutes. The advisor now walks into every meeting fully prepared and deeply connected.

12 Dealerships Unified Into Top Region Nationally

Twelve separate car dealerships. Twelve different reporting systems. Zero visibility into what was actually happening across the group. Leadership was making decisions based on guesswork.

Knight Ops built a unified KPI dashboard that consolidated every dealership's sales, service, and customer data into a single command center. For the first time, leadership could see performance in real time, spot underperformers instantly, and allocate resources where they mattered. The group went from middle-of-the-pack to the #1 performing region nationally.

Government Programs

CARES Act Applications Processed in 30 Days

When the CARES Act launched, organizations needed to process aid applications immediately. The problem: everything was on paper, and the volume was overwhelming.

Knight Ops built and deployed a complete paper-to-digital application system in 24 hours. Applicants submitted online, reviewers processed in a dashboard, and approvals triggered automated notifications. $4.5M in applications were facilitated within 30 days. Speed mattered — and the system delivered.
