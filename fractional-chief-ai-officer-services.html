The Role

What Does a Fractional Chief AI Officer Do?

A fractional Chief AI Officer provides the same strategic and technical leadership as a full-time Chief AI Officer, but on a retained, part-time basis. Instead of hiring someone at two hundred thousand to four hundred thousand dollars a year, you get senior AI leadership at a fraction of the cost with zero hiring risk.

At Knight Ops, your fractional Chief AI Officer is Daniel Knight, a builder with 10+ years of operational experience and 50+ AI systems deployed. Here is what the role covers:

  • AI Strategy and Roadmapping: Assess your entire business, identify the highest-ROI opportunities for AI, and build a prioritized plan to get there.
  • Custom System Design and Development: Build the dashboards, automations, agents, and integrations your business actually needs. No off-the-shelf tools that half-work.
  • Agent Orchestration: Design and deploy multi-agent systems using Claude, OpenAI, and custom MCP servers that handle complex workflows autonomously.
  • Ongoing Optimization: Tune prompts, monitor agent performance, A/B test automations, and continuously improve every system we deploy.
  • Team Enablement: If you have a dev team or virtual assistants, we train them on the AI systems so your organization builds internal capability over time.
  • Executive AI Briefings: Monthly reports showing what was built, what improved, and what the data says to build next.
Is This For You

Who Needs Fractional AI Officer Services?

Fractional AI officer services are for businesses that have outgrown basic automations but are not ready to hire a full-time AI executive. You are a fit if:

  • You run a business doing $500K or more in annual revenue and know AI can help you scale, but you are not sure where to start or what to prioritize.
  • You have tried tools like Zapier, ChatGPT, or off-the-shelf AI products but nothing integrates the way your business actually operates.
  • You need someone who understands both the technology and the business strategy, not just a developer who writes code in isolation.
  • You are a coach, consultant, agency owner, course creator, or service business founder who wants a custom AI tech stack that you own completely.
  • You want to move fast. You need AI systems deployed in days or weeks, not months of planning and procurement cycles.

We specifically serve coaches, consultants, agencies, course creators, speakers, and service businesses in the six-figure to eight-figure range. If you recognize that AI is a competitive advantage but do not have the in-house expertise to execute on it, a fractional Chief AI Officer closes that gap.
